✦ AI Features

Powered by Machine Learning

Not just a chatbot — a real ML recommendation engine

Engagement Analysis

Analyzes likes, comments, shares, and view duration across all videos to compute a weighted engagement score.

40% of match score

Niche Detection

NLP-based content classification identifies creator niche and matches it against brand product categories.

30% of match score

Budget Validation

ML model checks if brand marketing budget is within the investment cap based on company age and revenue data.

Auto validated

Audience Matching

Compares brand target audience demographics with creator audience data for maximum campaign relevance.

20% of match score

Creator Tier System

Automatically classifies creators as Micro, Mid, or Large tier based on follower count and engagement metrics.

Performance Score
